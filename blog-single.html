Little Starr International School Fun Fair 2024: A Day to Remember!

The much-anticipated Fun Fair at Little Starr International School, Mangalam, was a resounding success! Held on Sunday, 22nd December 2024, from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, this event brought together students, parents, and staff for a day filled with laughter, joy, and unforgettable memories.

A Feast for All Senses From delicious food varieties to engaging games, the fair catered to all age groups. Visitors indulged in mouthwatering cuisines and treats while exploring over 20 exciting games designed to challenge and entertain everyone.

Music and Merriment The highlight of the event was the Live DJ Music, which had everyone dancing to their heart’s content. The infectious beats and the vibrant atmosphere created a festive spirit that resonated with every attendee.

Fun Galore The fairgrounds were bustling with colorful attractions, including a Ferris wheel, carousel rides, and beautifully decorated stalls. Parents enjoyed free entry, while students enthusiastically participated in various activities, making the day truly special.

A Big Thank You! We extend our heartfelt gratitude to everyone who contributed to making this event a grand success. From the dedicated staff and volunteers to the enthusiastic participants, your efforts made this fair a cherished memory for all. We look forward to more events like this in the future, bringing our school community closer together. Stay tuned for updates, and don’t miss the next exciting celebration at Little Starr International School!
